A Picture Of Pablo Escobar

  • Dec. 1 1949 - Pablo Emilio Escobar Gaviria was born in Rionegro, a city within the department of antioquia in colombia. he had six other siblings.

  • july 1 1973 - then u.s president richard nixon signed off on the establishment of the drug enforcement agency or DEA. the goal was to have a central federal agency coordinate american's burgeoning effort to curb the import and use of illegal drugs.

  • 1970 - Escobar and the ochoa vasquez brothers, fabio, jorge luis and juan david, found the medellin cartel. prior to this, Escobar and his associates were already involved in the drug trade, smuggling cocaine into the u.s

  • march 1982 - while managing his criminal enterprises, Escobar funneled some of his ill-gottn gains to the community, building relationships with the poor and the local catholics. he was elected to the national congress as a member of the colombian liberal party, but was ousted somwtime later after the public officials accused him of having ties with the country's violent drug cartels.

  • march 9 1982 - about 3,748 pounds of cocaine was found in a tampa international airlines waarehouse at the miami airport. at the time, it was the largest seizure of cocaine in the u.s history and triggered a realization among law enforcementthat colombian criminal elements had banded together to ship larger amounts of their product.

  • april 30 1984 - an assasin working for escobar and the medellin cartel shot and killed colombian minister of justice rodrigo lara bonilla in bogota. bonilla was a vocal opponent of escobar, accusing him of having ties with drug cartels. he and others drove escobar out of office a year prior.

  • febuary 9 1985 - DEA agent enrique camarena was tortured and killed by members of the mexican guadalajara cartel. the investigstion into his death revealed juan matta=ballesteros, one of the leaders of the medellin cartel, was also involved in camarena's death.
